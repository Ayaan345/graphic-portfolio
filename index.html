About Me

I’m an emerging graphic designer who is actively building my skills through creating dummy logos, thumbnails, and other practice-based projects. Even though I’m at the beginning of my professional journey, I approach every design with intention, focusing on clarity, creativity, and strong visual impact.

I enjoy exploring branding, color theory, and layout design, and I consistently work on improving my ability to translate ideas into clean, meaningful visuals. My goal is to develop a portfolio that reflects both my growth and my dedication to thoughtful design.
